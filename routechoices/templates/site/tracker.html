GPS Trackers

Sharing your position

Sharing your position to provide live GPS tracking can be done in different ways.

You can use our smartphone application, or a modern Garmin watch loaded with our data field, or one of the supported dedicated GPS trackers.

In any case, your device is assigned a unique Routechoices "Device ID" that is used to identify your device with Routechoices.com. You will need it when entering events.

Read the specific instructions for your device type below.

Specific Instructions

Smartphone App

Instalation:

Install the app from the Google Play App on Android, or the Apple App Store on iOS
"Routechoices Tracker", by OneHertz.

How It Works:
  1. Open the app.
  2. Grant it access to high accuracy location at all time, as well as background execution permission. This ensures uninterrupted GPS tracking throughout your activity e.g. when your phone goes to sleep.
  3. Your 8 digits Device ID is then displayed in the center of the screen.
  4. Press "Start Live GPS" when you're ready to begin your run. The Device ID turns green when you are "Live".
  5. Go on your adventure!
  6. Press "Stop Live GPS" when you've finished.

Troubleshooting:
  • Ensure that high-accuracy GPS is enabled in your phone's system settings.
  • Recent Android versions may restrict background processes to improve battery life, which can affect apps like Routechoices Tracker. To ensure continuous tracking, add Routechoices Tracker to battery optimization exceptions in your device settings.
  • Some Android manufacturers have additional battery-saving settingsâ€”make sure to disable them or add the app to their exceptions list.
  • iOS restricts applications that can run in the background, make sure that background execution is allowed and Routechoices Tracker has permission to access location services at all times.
  • iOS also limits when an application can fetch location data, ensure that the privacy setting for location access in "Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Routechoices Tracker" is set to "Always"

Garmin Watches App

Instalation:

Install the data field for Garmin watches from the Garmin Connect IQ App Store
"Routechoices Tracker", by OneHertz.

How It Works:
  1. Add the data field to the activity profiles in which you want to be tracked.
  2. Start an activity with the profile contaning the data field.
  3. The data field do displays your 8 digits Device ID next to the status of connection with the server (OFFLINE/LIVE).
  4. You will be sending location data to Routechoices.com server as soon, and as long, as your watch connects with bluetooth to the Garmin Mobile App on a phone that has network data, this no matter if the data field is visible or not.
  5. Stop the activity and return to the main screen to stop sending your location data.

Dedicated GPS Trackers

Supported devices:
  • GT06 protocol devices
  • Mictrack devices
  • Queclink devices
  • Teltonika devices
  • Tracktape devices
  • Xexun protocol devices
  • Xexun2 protocol devices

Own a product not listed above? Enquire us about adding support for its protocol!

Configuration:

Configure the devices to share data over TCP with the server at address routechoices.com on the following port:

  • 5023: GT06
  • 5191: Mictrack
  • 5004: Queclink
  • 5027: Teltonika
  • 2003: TrackTape
  • 5006: Xexun
  • 5233: Xexun2
The recommended data push interval is 5 seconds, and the recommended GPS data acquisition interval is 1 second, but feel free to adjust the settings depending on your usage as this settings affect the battery life.
