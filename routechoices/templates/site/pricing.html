Frequently asked questions

about Routechoices.com subscriptions and billing

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No. We won't ask for any payment information until you decide to subscribe to a plan.

Will I get a trial period to test the features?

Yes. It's enabled automatically when you create your first club. It does not require a credit card and lasts for 10 days. When the Free Trial is over, you can continue with a paid plan.

Is there a minimum subscription period?

The minimum subscription period is 1 month. You are free to cancel its renewal at any time. Your subscription will be active until the end of the period you paid for.

What happen when I end my subscription?

Once your subscription expire, at the end of the paying period, you will not be able to edit old events or create new events, all your past events will stay online.

My orienteering club is a non profit organisation, do I have to pay something?

No. The Routechoices Live GPS platform main goal is to promote orienteering. Hence if you qualify as a non commercial orienteering organisation (associated with the International Orienteering Federation), you can use {{ site.name }} completely for free. Just create a club and fill the Orienteering Club Request in the settings to get started. The Routechoices.com team reserves the right to judge who qualify and can revoke your status depending on your usage at any moment.

Do each club member need a subscription?

No. The members you assign to your club have their contributions included your club plan.

Do I need to pay something to watch events?

No. The organisers are the only one required to pay something. All the spectators have the same access to the events.
